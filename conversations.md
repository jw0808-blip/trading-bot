# TraderJoes AI Conversation Log

All conversations with Grok and Claude in the "TraderJoes Trading Firm" project, plus all bot activity, are automatically logged here.

**How to add entries:**
- Bot activity is logged automatically
- Post to `POST /log` on the ai-logger service with your LOGGER_WEBHOOK_SECRET
- Use `!log <message>` in Discord to manually log conversation snippets
